AQI Chất lượng Ảnh hưởng sức khỏe
0-50 Tốt Không ảnh hưởng đến sức khỏe
51-100 Trung bình Air quality is acceptable; however, for some pollutants there may be a moderate health concern for a very small number of people. For example, people who are unusually sensitive to ozone may experience respiratory symptoms.
101-150 Kém Unhealthy for sensitive groups: members of sensitive groups may experience health effects. The general public is not likely to be affected.
151-200 Xấu Unhealthy: everyone may begin to experience health effects; members of sensitive groups may experience more serious health effects
201-300 Rất xấu Very unhealthy: health warnings of emergency conditions. The entire population is more likely to be affected.
300+ Nguy hại Hazardous: Health alert: everyone may experience more serious health effects
